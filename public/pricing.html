Common questions

What is a credit?
One credit equals one AI tutoring interaction. Each time your child sends a message and receives a response, that uses one credit. Photo-based questions also use one credit.
Can I change plans later?
Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade at any time. Changes take effect at the start of your next billing cycle. Unused credits don't carry over between months.
How does the free trial work?
Every plan starts with a 14-day free trial and 10 credits. No payment information is required to start. If you don't subscribe after the trial, your account simply pauses.
Is my child's data safe?
Absolutely. We encrypt all data in transit and at rest. Chat transcripts are retained for 90 days, then permanently deleted. We never share data with third parties. Read our privacy policy for full details.
