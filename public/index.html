MathBuddy — Grade 4
What is 48 divided by 6?
Great question! Let's think about it together. Division means splitting into equal groups. If you have 48 cookies and want to share them equally among 6 friends, how many does each friend get?
Hmm, I don't know...
That's okay! Let's start smaller. Do you know what 6 x 5 is? Try counting: 6, 12, 18, 24, 30... So 6 x 5 = 30. Now, what's 6 x 8?
